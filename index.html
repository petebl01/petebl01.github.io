Program Director: Jane Peterson

Cell: (218) 330-5932 Email: JPeterson@clcmn.edu

Jane Peterson is among the most successful in the National Junior College Athletic Association.
She has coached 21 All-Americans at Central Lakes College. 2014 is her 24th season at CLC, where she
has led teams to three national titles and ten appearances in the NJCAA DIII National Tournament.
Her CLC teams have earned seven conference championships. Jane is a three-time National Coach
of the Year (2000, 2001, 2007). Her record through 23 seasons is 536-223. Raider volleyball teams
under Coach Peterson have been in the national rankings on a consistent basis, having earned their
way to the NJCAA tournament 9 of the last 13 years.

The 2013 team finished with a 29-3 record. Also in 2013 Peterson was named the AVCA
(American Volleyball Coaches Association) Two Year College West Region Coach of the Year.
The 2012 squad earned Peterson her 500th win. Also in 2012 she was inducted into the NJCAA
Volleyball Coaches Hall of Fame. After taking the Raiders to the national championship in 2007,
Coach Peterson was named the 2007 Two-Year College Southwest Region Coach of the Year and the
2007 Two-Year College National Coach of the Year by the AVCA. A California native, Jane was a
Hall of Fame athlete at the College of William and Mary before earning her master's degree in
athletic administration from St. Cloud State University. Prior to becoming head coach at CLC,
she was an SCSU assistant volleyball coach.

About Central Lakes Juniors

We provide a comfortable and competetive environment for student athletes in middle
school and high school. Not only will our participants gain skill on the volleyball
court, they will learn values such as hard work and hospitality. Our club teams travel
to multiple tournaments in a given season. We also host tournaments frequently throughout
the winter months. For more information contact Jane Peterson.
